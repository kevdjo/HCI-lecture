WHO WE ARE

M&T is a multinational clothing company originated from Indonesia.

Our goals is to create and provide timeless, modern, and up-to-date
menswear clothes. With that goal in mind, we hope that we can help
men dress better one garment at a time.

OUR HISTORY

Our company begins in 1996, by our founder Kevin Djong who invented this company in his house with his friend William Amadeus. with their passion to improve the fashion style in Indonesia, they started to create clothing designs for teenagers and young adults. At first they rent a house to start their business operating system that is located in Jakarta.
